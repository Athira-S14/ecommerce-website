About Us

Athira Boutique

With names like AND, Global Desi, AND Girl, Global Desi Girl, and itse on its brand roster; Ochre & Black Private Limited is an esteemed organization in the fashion circuit today. From housing the sophistication of western wear with AND & AND Girl, to lending your ethnic look a vibrant touch with Global Desi and Global Desi Girl — each brand under Ochre & Black Private Limited harbours its own persona. In 2021, the fashion house launched itse, a new brand that caters to a young market looking for ethnic styles at a budget-friendly price.

BRAND PROFILE

Found in 1995, AND stemmed out of the realization that the modern Indian woman didn’t have too many contemporary silhouettes to choose from. Bridging this gap ever since, today AND has garnered the recognition of a brand that makes global styles for the contemporary woman, understanding the sensibilities of fashion, comfort, quality, and style effortlessly

GIRL BRAND PROFILE

Often appealing and catering to a sect that is progressive, ambitious, and live a wholesome life; the label is ever-involving while keeping itself aligned with the needs of the urban Indian woman.
